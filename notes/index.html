Spring 2025

Intermediate Quantum Mechanics I

Notes for the course Intermediate Quantum Mechanics I, taught by Prof. Dr. Ben Heidenreich at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. The notes are based on the brilliant lectures and the book "Modern Quantum Mechanics" by J. J. Sakurai and Jim Napolitano. The notes are not meant to be a complete summary of the course, but rather a collection of concepts and derivations I found useful.
