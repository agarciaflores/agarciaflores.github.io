The Price of this Site

Studying advanced mathematics implies you are likely not experiencing hunger, cold, or sickness. This site is available to you at no cost for your use. If you find this site valuable, please consider making a contribution to help alleviate suffering in the world.

You might donate money to organizations like Unicef, Doctors without Borders, Partners in Health, Oxfam, or a similar group in your country. You might also consider volunteering your time and expertise instead.

MathWise is a compilation of my academic notes during my time as an undergraduate student at Arizona State University. I learned from one of my favorite physics professors that writing a textbook on a subject is the greatest way to learn it. Here's my attempt at it.

I aim to utilize these notes in my own courses someday. Until then, I hope that someone, somewhere, finds them beneficial. I wish you success and hope you will one day explore uncharted waters.
