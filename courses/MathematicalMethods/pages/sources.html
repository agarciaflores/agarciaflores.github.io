Sources

I do not wish to convey that all the material on this site is derived from an epiphany. It was in fact gathered from a variety of textbooks, courses, and experiences. When it comes to theorems, of course they've come from somewhere. Instead of tracing down the first person to every prove a theorem, I give credit to the person who introduced me to the theorem. I.e., the textbook I read the theorem in.
